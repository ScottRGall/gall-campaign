Nixlet

Sea Sprite Pet: Nixlet, the Tideglimmer

Creature Type: Tiny Fey (Aquatic)

Alignment: Usually Chaotic Good (but deeply loyal once bonded)

Movement: 20 ft. walk, 40 ft. swim, 30 ft. fly (only above water or in misty sea-spray)

Appearance

Nixlet is a palm-sized sea sprite with smooth sea-glass skin that shifts between teal, pearl, and moonlit blue. Their hair floats like kelp even on land, and their wings look like thin fins made of shimmering water membrane—almost invisible unless they catch the light. Their eyes are huge and curious, like a tidepool at midnight. They wear a tiny sash made of braided seaweed and carry a needle-thin coral spear no longer than a sewing needle.

Personality

Nixlet is:

  • Nosy and fearless (will investigate everything)
  • Protective of their cleric in a “small creature, big attitude” way
  • Easily offended by disrespect toward the ocean (or dolphins)
  • A little dramatic—gasps loudly, clutches pearls, makes silent judgment faces
  • They don’t always understand “indoor voices,” and they’ll absolutely steal shiny items “for safekeeping.”

Bond With the Sea Elf Cleric

A sea sprite doesn’t become a pet the way a dog does—it becomes a chosen companion.  Nixlet bonded with Tally after witnessing one of these moments.  She healed a dying dolphin after a storm churned poison into the bay.  After that, Nixlet followed her for three nights, watching, judging, then finally declaring, “You’re… acceptable. I will allow you to be mine.”

Tideglow

  • Nixlet can glow softly like moonlight on waves, lighting up a small area when needed.

Salt-Sense

They can “taste” the air/water and tell if danger is near:

  • blood in the water
  • poison
  • undead taint
  • storm pressure building

 Bubble Whisper

  • The Nixlet can create tiny bubbles that carry faint sound—useful for short-range secret messages underwater.

What They Want (and will absolutely demand)

Nixlet expects:

  • a small dish of fresh saltwater each night
  • to sleep in your hood, hair, or pocket like a cat
  • to be included in rituals, because they are “basically clergy too”

Pet “Habits” That Make Them Feel Real

  • Leaves damp footprints everywhere and acts like that’s your problem
  • Hates dry air and will perch in a waterskin like it’s a hot tub
  • Laughs silently at serious people
  • Will absolutely gossip with fish

Nicknames for their “Big Person” 

  • Tally - when other people are around
  • Tide Keeper - when they are alone
  • Sea-sister - when she is feeling affectionate 
  • Frown-fish - when trying to cheer her up 
  • Dry-brain - when she is miffed with her

Nixlet, the Tideglimmer

Armor Class 15 (natural grace)

Hit Points 13 (3d4 + 6)

Speed 20 ft., swim 40 ft., fly 30 ft. (only above water or in misty sea-spray)

STR 4 (-3)            INT 10 (+0) 

DEX 18 (+4)         WIS 14 (+2) 

CON 14 (+2)       CHA 13 (+1) 

Saving Throws Dex +6, Wis +4

Skills Perception +4, Stealth +6, Insight +4, Acrobatics +6

Damage Resistances cold; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks (optional—use if you want them tougher)

Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14

Languages understands Common and Sylvan; can speak in squeaks, clicks, and watery whispers (can speak normally while in water)

Proficiency Bonus +2

Trait

Needle-Coral Spear

  • Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  • Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage

Tidepin Dart (Recharge 5–6)

  • Ranged Spell Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature.
  • Hit: 7 (2d4 + 2) cold damage, and the target’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of Nixlet’s next turn.

Bonus Actions

Bubble Whisper: Nixlet creates a cluster of tiny bubbles that carries a faint sound message to a creature within 60 feet underwater that can see the bubbles. The message can be up to six words long.

Slip Into the Spray:  If Nixlet is within 5 feet of water, fog, heavy mist, or sea-spray, they can move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

Reactions

Small Creature, Big Attitude (1/Short Rest)

  • When a creature Nixlet can see within 30 feet targets Tally (or Nixlet’s bonded companion) with an attack, Nixlet can flare with moonlit tideglow and spit a furious string of bubbles.
  • The attacker must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or have disadvantage on the triggering attack roll.

Bonded Companion: Chosen, Not Owned

Nixlet can bond to one willing creature (usually Tally). While bonded:

  • Nixlet always knows the bonded creature’s direction if within 1 mile, as long as both are on the same plane.
  • If the bonded creature is underwater, Nixlet can communicate with them telepathically in simple emotions + images (not full speech).
  • If the bond is willingly broken, Nixlet leaves dramatically.

Running Nixlet at the Table (simple rules)

  • Nixlet acts on Tally’s initiative (same turn), but has their own actions/bonus/reaction.
  • Nixlet won’t usually deal killing blows unless the party wants that tone.
  • Nixlet prefers scouting, disruption, warnings, and protecting Tally

Optional: “Pet Chaos” Quirk (Fun Mechanic)

Once per long rest, if a shiny object is seen and unattended, the DM may call for a DC 12 Wisdom (Animal Handling or Persuasion) check from Tally. On a fail, Nixlet acquires it “for safekeeping.”
